Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 10, 2025

GrantComply uses Google APIs to access only the specific documents you explicitly select through our document picker. We can read, analyze, download these selected documents as PDFs, and add AI-generated comments for compliance suggestions. We do not store, share, or sell any personal or document data.

Data Access

When you connect your Google account, our app can only access documents that you explicitly select through our document picker interface. We can read these selected documents, download them as PDFs, and add comments with AI-generated compliance suggestions. This limited access is used only to provide AI-powered compliance analysis and suggestions for your grant documents.

Data Storage

We do not permanently store document contents. Metadata (such as filenames or processing timestamps) may be temporarily cached for app functionality and deleted after processing.

User Consent

All access to your Google account occurs through Google's OAuth 2.0 protocol. You can revoke access anytime at Google Account Permissions.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data during transmission and processing. All communications with Google APIs are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols.

Third-Party Services

GrantComply integrates with Google Drive and Google Docs through official Google APIs. Your use of these services is also governed by Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
